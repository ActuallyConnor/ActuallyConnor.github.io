VRPlayin

Our mission is simple: to bring incredible virtual experiences to everyone. Combining top notch customer service and premium technology in a clean, contemporary setting, we at VRPlayin will do whatever it takes to ensure that everyone experiences the mind-blowing potential of virtual reality.

 

At VRPlayin you can expect a clean modern space complete with a dedicated VR station for your personal use, a viewing bar for friends to share the experience and a Customer Care Representative to offer you tips, tricks, and how-to support throughout the duration of your session.

 

We have 19 stations available, semi-private or private areas for groups, a driving simulator, green screen room and a VR experience catalogue loaded with something for everyone. Our knowledgeable and passionate staff is why VRPlayin is Toronto’s premier location for virtual reality!

What We Offer

Gaming tournaments that are traditionally hosted at the Microsoft Stores, but what we are offering will change that. As stated above, our virtual reality experience centre offers something that Microsoft does not have access to; we have a place where people can play VR together in a mass setting.

 

The benefits of having access to something like this means that Microsoft can begin to really get into the virtual reality gaming scene with big tournaments and access to new and unreleased games. Because of our unique relationship with different game developers we have access to unreleased games such as the one that will be used for this tournament.

What We Expect

VRPlayin is looking for a potential partnership with Microsoft where we would become the GTA Microsoft Stores’ exclusive virtual reality venue for all HTC Vive virtual reality related events.

 

There are quite a few benefits that we can offer to Microsoft, such as: a location with 19 VR headsets available to them for any VR related events which they wish to host, excellent staff that has the dedication and knowledge to run any events that may be upcoming, and access to our commercially licensed full catalogue of games.

 

Any social media posts done by VRPlayin can include Microsoft as a partner in the event. What we are asking for in this partnership is for Microsoft to publish any events that are in partner with Microsoft on their different social media platforms, and to fully provide or in part provide funding for a prize that would be given at the events.

 

For all the events, we would also welcome Microsoft to send an employee to our location during the events so they may promote their store, their product, and any role they’d like play in the event. Our goal is to establish  a mutually beneficial relationship with Microsoft.

The Event

VRPlayin highly recommends to host the event on Friday August 11, 2017 from 5pm to 12am. All bookings will be blocked in advance for those times to ensure that the event runs as smoothly as possible.

 

The game that VRPlayin proposing for the event is, Sprint Vector, developed by Survios. On of the biggest and pioneer of VR game developers. They developed the first ever co-op VR shooter game Raw Data, which  reached number one overall on Steam's global top sellers list in September 2016, it was also the first consumer VR game to reach $1 million in sales in a month. Sprint Vector is another big title that just announced in E3 and VRPlayin is able to secure an exclusive partnership with Survios prior to the official release.  More Information about Sprint Vector can be found here, Raw Data here, and information about the developer Survios here.

 

“Welcome to Sprint Vector championship intergalactica, where the physical thrill of extreme sports meets the unhinged energy of a crazy game show! Run, jump, climb, fling, and fly at extreme velocity as you race up to eight players and battle obstacles in this frenetic VR adrenaline platformer”, Survios’ Steam description.

During the event day, VRPlayin will provide the 10 HTC Vive stations in the back of the venue for participants for practices matches before their official matches. The 5 stations in the front will be for official time trial runs for the event. In addition, VRPlayin will have several employees on site to provide support such as keeping track of the contenders and marking down times. The gaming event especially the matches will be livestreamed in the front on Twitch and Youtube. Attendance for this inaugural event is free for anyone to play.

 

Survios will send one of their staff members on site to provide technical support in the case of any software malfunctions. And they will also provide marketing support such promoting the event on their social media accounts. More details will be determined. The Microsoft booth will either be set up at the front by the window or in the middle between the practice runs and the official runs.

 

An event without a host is not fun. To maximize the competitiveness and excitement for the spectators, VRPlayin will be inviting a commenter to host the livestream event, and this commenter will be selected from one of the popular Toronto Youtubers. The link to the livestream will be provided to Microsoft in advance. VRPlayin definitely would like the Yorkdale mall location to livestream the event. In addition,  VRPlayin would greatly appreciate if that link could be shared to all the Microsoft stores so that they have the choice of livestreaming the event as well.

 

VRPlayin will also be in contact with various local internet cafes, e-sports lounges, and gaming spaces to livestream the event.

Prizes

VRPlayin will provide prizes to the first, second, and third place contestants.

 

  • First place will receive 10 VR sessions ($400 value)
  • Second place will receive 5 VR sessions ($200 value)
  • Third place will receive 3 VR sessions ($120 value)

 

VRPlayin requests that Microsoft to match the cash value of the prizes that VRPlayin is offering to maximize the attraction for this inaugural event.  Survios will be providing us with copies of the game for the event and we are in discussion with them to provide support when it comes to prizing as well.
